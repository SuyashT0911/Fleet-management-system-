# Fleet-management-system-

📌 Overview

The Fleet Management System (FMS) is designed to efficiently manage a fleet of vehicles. It enables tracking, scheduling, maintenance management, fuel monitoring, and reporting to improve operational efficiency and reduce costs.

---

🧩 Core Modules

🚘 Vehicle Management

- Manage vehicle records, types, and availability
- Track vehicle status and specifications

👨‍✈️ Driver Management

- Maintain driver profiles and performance
- Assign drivers to trips and schedules

🗺️ Trip Scheduling

- Plan and optimize routes
- Assign vehicles and drivers efficiently

🛠️ Maintenance Management

- Schedule maintenance and inspections
- Track incidents and maintenance history

⛽ Fuel Tracking

- Monitor fuel consumption
- Analyze fuel efficiency and costs

💳 Payment & Invoicing

- Process payments for trips
- Generate invoices and track transactions

📊 Reporting Module

- Analyze vehicle usage and performance
- Generate custom reports and insights

---

🗄️ Database Design

The system includes structured entities such as:

- Vehicle
- Driver
- Route
- Trip
- Maintenance
- Fuel
- Payment
- Invoice
- Customer
- Insurance
- Feedback

Relationships include:

- One-to-Many (Vehicle → Trips, Driver → Trips)
- Many-to-One (Trip → Vehicle, Trip → Driver)
- Many-to-Many (Driver ↔ Incident)

---

🔄 Workflow Highlights

- Vehicle and driver registration
- Trip scheduling with availability checks
- Maintenance and incident tracking
- Fuel logging and analysis
- Payment processing and invoice generation
- Report generation and analytics
